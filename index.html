WHO WE ARE

A Holding Company Built for Generational Impact

Lumen Holdings LLC operates as a strategic umbrella organization overseeing a diverse portfolio of operating companies. Our structure is anchored by The Wilkins Family Trust, ensuring long-term stability, asset protection, and strategic continuity across generations.

We combine disciplined capital allocation with operational expertise across technology, education, and international trade. Each entity within our portfolio operates autonomously while benefiting from shared resources, strategic guidance, and financial infrastructure.
